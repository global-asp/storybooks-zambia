Tala tunyama.

Look at the animals.

Tamba inama.

Ngombe echi kuhanjika, “Mooo.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

Ing’ombe aiti, “moo.”

Pembe echi kuhanjika, “Meh, meh.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

Imbushi aiti, “meh, meh.”

Mukoko echi kuhanjikanga “Neee.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

Ihoshi aliti, “neei.”

Ngulu echi kuhanjikanga, “Nguuu nguu.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

Inkumba aiti, “ng’a, ng’a.”

Kasumbi echi kuhanjikanga, “Kokokokooo.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

Inkoko aiti, “ko, ko, ko.”

Kawa echi kuhanjikanga, “Woo woo woo.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

Imbwa aiti, “bwa, bwa.”

Kulimi echi kuhanjikanga, “Shhh!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

Shibulimi ati, “shii.”
