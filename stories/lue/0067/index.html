Ngwechi kusonga jitamba

I peel the potatoes.

Mpala imfyumbu.

Ngwechi kuvatula kabeji

I chop a cabbage.

Mputaula kabeci.

Ngwechi kukwala ma kaloti.

I grate the carrots.

Nsesa kalota.

Ngwechi kukosa makunde.

I wash the beans.

Nsamba cilemba.

Ngwechi kuvatula chelu

I cut the butternut.

Mputula utupushi.

Ngwechi kuteta mafo.

I chop the spinach.

Mputula bashipinaci.

Mama yami echi kuteta sapola.

My mom chops the onions.

Bamaayo baputula onyoni.

Sapola yechi kungulilisa nge vanayivatula.

Onions make me cry when they are chopped.

Onyono ilenga ukuti nalila nga ileeputulwa.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Masho Kaloza
Language: Luvale
Level: Level 1
