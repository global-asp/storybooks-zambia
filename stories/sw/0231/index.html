Hakuna mvua.

It is dry.

Taileloka.

Jua linang’aa.

The sun is hot.

Namukaba mukasuba.

Kuna upepo.

It is windy.

Umwela uleepuupa.

Kuna mawingu.

It is cloudy.

Kuli amakumbi.

Kuna baridi.

It is cold.

Nakutalala.

Mvua inanyesha.

It is raining.

Ileeloka.

Kuna ngurumo za radi.

There is thunder.

Ileepanta.

Naona upinde wa mvua.

I see a rainbow.

Namona umukolamfula.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Sandra McDougall, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Mutugi Kamundi
Language: Kiswahili
Level: Level 1
