Tazama wanyama.

Look at the animals.

Tamba inama.

Ng’ombe anasema, “Moo.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

Ing’ombe aiti, “moo.”

Mbuzi anasema, “Mee, mee.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

Imbushi aiti, “meh, meh.”

Farasi anasema, “Nei.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

Ihoshi aliti, “neei.”

Nguruwe anasema, “Ngruu.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

Inkumba aiti, “ng’a, ng’a.”

Kuku anasema, “Ko, ko, ko.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

Inkoko aiti, “ko, ko, ko.”

Mbwa anasema, “Wouh, wouh.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

Imbwa aiti, “bwa, bwa.”

Mkulima anasema, “Nyamaza!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

Shibulimi ati, “shii.”
