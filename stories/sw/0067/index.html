Ninamenya viazi.

I peel the potatoes.

Mpala imfyumbu.

Ninakatakata kabichi.

I chop a cabbage.

Mputaula kabeci.

Ninasaga karoti.

I grate the carrots.

Nsesa kalota.

Ninaosha maharagwe.

I wash the beans.

Nsamba cilemba.

Ninakata maboga.

I cut the butternut.

Mputula utupushi.

Ninakatakata sukuma-wiki.

I chop the spinach.

Mputula bashipinaci.

Mama yangu anakatakata vitunguu.

My mom chops the onions.

Bamaayo baputula onyoni.

Vitunguu hunifanya nilie vikiwa vinakatwakatwa.

Onions make me cry when they are chopped.

Onyono ilenga ukuti nalila nga ileeputulwa.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
