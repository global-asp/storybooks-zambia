Oghu muthithi ne ghure.

This dress is long.

Ilaya ili liitali.

Oyu djeshi ne mukuru.

This jersey is big.

Sweta iyi ikulu.

Odhi ndjato ne dhikuru.

This bag is big.

Icoola ici ciikulu.

Oghu muya ne…

This belt is…

Umushipi uyu…

Oyu hutji ne mumbiru.

This hat is small.

Icisote ici ciinoono.

Oyi yikamishi ne yifupi.

The socks are short.

Insokoshi shiipi.

Ene agha makuha ne mapya.

But these shoes are new.

Nomba insapato ishi shiipya.

… kuni rikanya thiwana.

… they fit well.

… Shilalinga bwino.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
