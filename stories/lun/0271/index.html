Tumakasa tuyedi twakukwata nachu.

Two little hands to hold.

Utumaboko tubili utwakwikatilako.

Tunyendu tuyedi twakwasa nachu.

Two little feet to kick.

Utumakasa tubili utwakupantilako.

Tumensu tuyedi twakumona nachu.

Two little eyes to see.

Utumenso tubili utwakumwenako.

Tumatu tuyedi twakutiyisha nachu.

Two little ears to hear.

Utumatwi tubili utwakumfwilako.

Na makasa ayedi akukumbata nachu.

And two loving arms to HUG!

Utumaboko tubili utwacitemwiko utwakukumbatilako.

Written by: Carole Bloch
Illustrated by: Richard MacIntosh
Translated by: Oscar Zangata
Language: Lunda
Level: Level 1
