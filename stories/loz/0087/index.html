Ndayanda Kubala I like to read! Ninapenda kusoma!

Letta Machoga

Wiehan de Jager

Lusaka Teachers

SiLozi

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Nibata kubala.

I like to read.

Nalitemwa ukubelenga.

Kimañi yonikabalela?

Who can I read to?

Naani nalaabelengela?

Kezelaka ulobezi.

My sister is asleep.

Nkashi yandi nalaala.

Kimañi yonikabalela?

Who can I read to?

Naani nalaabelengela?

Boma nibo kuku babasali bapatehile.

My mother and grandmother are busy.

Bamaayo nabamaama bali nencito.

Kimañi yonikabalela?

Who can I read to?

Naani nalaabelengela?

Bo ndate nibo kuku babana bapatehile.

My father and grandfather are busy.

Bataata na bashikulu bali nencito.

Kimañi yonikabalela? Nikaipalela ninosi!

Who can I read to? I can read to myself!

Naani nalaabelengela? Kanshi nalaibelengelafye nemwine!

Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Lusaka Teachers
