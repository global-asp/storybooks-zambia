Nalitemwa Ukubelenga! I like to read! Ninapenda kusoma!

Letta Machoga

Wiehan de Jager

Agnes Chileshe Chibamba

IciBemba

Level 1

Nalitemwa ukubelenga.

I like to read.

Nalitemwa ukubelenga.

Kuti na belengela nani Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Who can I read to?

Kuti na belengela nani Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Nkashi yandi nasendama.

My sister is asleep.

Nkashi yandi nasendama.

Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Who can I read to?

Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Bamayo na bamama bali nemilimo.

My mother and grandmother are busy.

Bamayo na bamama bali nemilimo.

Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Who can I read to?

Bushe nani nalabelengela?

Batata na bashikulu bali nemilimo.

My father and grandfather are busy.

Batata na bashikulu bali nemilimo.

Bushe nani nalabelengela? Nalaibelengela nemwine!

Who can I read to? I can read to myself!

Bushe nani nalabelengela? Nalaibelengela nemwine!

Written by: Letta Machoga
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Agnes Chileshe Chibamba
Language: IciBemba
Level 1
Source: I like to read from African Storybook
