Mbena kwimba.

I am singing.

Ndeimba.

Ubena kushajikapo.

She is waving.

Aleesokonya.

Mbena kutota.

I am clapping.

Ndetoota.

Ubena kwi yolola.

She is stretching.

Aleeyolola.

Ubena kwita.

He is calling.

Aleita.

Mbena ku kumbula.

I am answering.

Ndeyasuka.

Ubena kunvwa.

She is listening.

Aleekutika.

Ubena kuba ka?

What are you doing?

Finshi uleecita?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Ruth Kapamba & Mwitila Ntabo
Language: Kaonde
Level: Level 1
