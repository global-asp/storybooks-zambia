Mbuka naku banza mujilo.

I wake up and make fire.

Ndabuka na ukukosha umulilo.

Nakabisha ne mema.

I boil some water.

Ndakafya amenshi.

Na tetaula nenkunyi.

I chop the firewood.

Ndateba inkuni.

Na kumbaula nemu mpoto.

I stir the pot.

Ndepika.

Na pyanga nepanshi.

I sweep the floor.

Ndapyanga.

Na ovwa nemasanyi.

I wash the dishes.

Ukusuka imbale.

Kika kyongingijila bibye nyi… bino nkasami aye sakayatu?

Why do I work so hard… … when my brother is busy playing?

Mulandunshi mbombesesha ifi… Elo ninshi munyinane ena aleangalafye?

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Ruth Kapamba, Mwitila Ntabo
Language: Kaonde
Level: Level 1
